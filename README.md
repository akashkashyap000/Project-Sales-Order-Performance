Sales Order Dashboard (2020–2025)
Project Overview

This project presents an end-to-end Sales Analysis Dashboard designed to help business stakeholders track performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions. The solution covers sales, profit, quantity, regional performance, category insights, and payment behavior.

The project follows an industry-style analytics workflow including BRD, SQL analysis, RCA, business insights, and dashboard visualization.

Business Problem

No consolidated view of sales and profit performance

Difficulty in tracking monthly trends and seasonality

Limited visibility into top-performing states and categories

Lack of insights into profitability drivers and payment preferences

Objectives

Monitor overall sales and profit KPIs

Analyze monthly sales trends over time

Identify top-performing states and categories

Evaluate profitability at category and sub-category level

Understand customer payment behavior

Key KPIs

Total Revenue

Total Profit

Profit Margin (%)

Total Quantity Sold

Dashboard Features

KPI summary section for quick performance snapshot

Monthly sales trend analysis (Line Chart)

Category-wise monthly sales contribution (Treemap)

Top 5 states by sales (Horizontal Bar Chart)

Profit analysis by category & sub-category (Treemap)

Quantity vs Profit relationship (Scatter Plot)

Payment-mode contribution analysis (Pie Chart)

Interactive filters for Year, Category, State, and Payment Mode

Root Cause Analysis (RCA)

Identified sales volatility due to seasonality and promotions

Detected low-margin sub-categories impacting overall profitability

Observed revenue concentration in limited states

Found dependency on specific payment modes

Business Insights

Business operates at a healthy 26%+ profit margin

Electronics category is the primary revenue driver

A small number of states contribute the majority of sales

Higher quantity generally increases profit, with some diminishing returns

Digital payment modes dominate customer transactions
